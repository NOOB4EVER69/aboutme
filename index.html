About Me

Hey, I’m Mustapha — but online, I’m known as NOOB4 EVER.

I’m not just another dev with a keyboard — I’m a chaotic creative force with a love for breaking the rules (but in a smart way). I build things that feel like dreams, glitches, emotions, or sometimes all at once. Whether it's an HTML site with a heartbeat, a weird little game that makes people cry, or a profile maker that feels like a song — I bring raw personality into every line of code.

I’m Egyptian, born in 2007, and I’ve been exploring tech, emotion, and the weird corners of the internet since I was a kid. I love turning messy ideas into something real. If it’s deep, nostalgic, dark, random, or too weird for the mainstream — it’s probably my thing.

What I do:

  • Mental health tools that feel personal and honest
  • HTML/JS projects that play with identity, emotion, and creativity
  • Scratch games with stories that hit hard
  • Creative image-to-sound experiments
  • Sites that feel like a vibe, not just a page

Things I love:

  • Dark colors, glitchy energy, and music that makes you feel
  • Mixing art with tech — storytelling with logic
  • Making stuff that people don’t forget
  • Giving others tools to express themselves

I’m not here to follow trends. I’m here to start something.
